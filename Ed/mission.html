The mission is to assure a sense of warmth, comfort, and dignity to the less fortunate during the colder months of the year and to hopefully make a bigger impact on the nation in dealing with such an unreasonable issue.

According to the National Library of Medicine the US alone sees nearly 25,000 deaths each year related to hypothermia. Although shelters are available during this season, sometimes that is not enough. If we as ohioans can provide such an amount of warmth during this season to cut that number down by 1000, we would still be saving 1000 lives together.

All thats needed is the donation of a coat, hoodie, or sweater and we can begin to progress on changing these numbers more significantly.
